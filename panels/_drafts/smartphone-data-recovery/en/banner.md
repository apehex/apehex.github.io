When all is lost...
Take the chip off the SP motherboard, with hot air or an IR heater.
Generally eMMC chip.
Tear down a SD card adaptater.
OR buy a socket board (KZ sthg).
Solder / connect the chip.
Plug into linux & read with a hex / fs cli.
